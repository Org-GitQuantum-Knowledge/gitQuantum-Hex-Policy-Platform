## Terminology

### Official Terms
- **gitQuantum**: The product name (not "HEX Policy Platform")
- **Quantum Engine**: The core policy evaluation engine
- **Baseline Evaluator**: The comparison/reference evaluator
- **Quantum Time**: Performance metric for gitQuantum
- **Baseline Time**: Performance metric for reference implementation
- **Performance Factor**: Ratio of baseline to quantum time

### Deprecated Terms
- ❌ HEX Policy Platform
- ❌ HEX VM
- ❌ OPA Simulator
- ❌ OPA Time
